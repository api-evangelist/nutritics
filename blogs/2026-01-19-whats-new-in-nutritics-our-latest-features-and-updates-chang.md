<li><strong>Update to Gluten Allergen Handling:</strong> Where a gluten allergen without any cereal subspecies is listed, the allergen scanner will now trigger all cereals to be tagged, rather than ‘Gluten’.</li>



<li><strong>Supplier Portal:</strong> A new ‘Supplier no longer active’ warning will show on published data from expired suppliers.&nbsp;</li>



<li><strong>Quickdish:</strong> You can now include dietary preferences (e.g., Gluten-Free, Vegan, Halal, Kosher) directly on printed dockets.</li>



<li><strong>Translations: </strong>A viewer’s translation setting is now applied to shared menus (rather than the owners).</li>



<li><strong>Insights:</strong> New KPI line and label now available for charts. Pagination tooltip and KPI data is also extended.</li>
</ul>
