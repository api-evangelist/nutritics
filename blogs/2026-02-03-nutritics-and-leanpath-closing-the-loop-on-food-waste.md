<p>Kitchens generate a massive amount of data, but too often, that data lives in silos. Today, we are changing that.&nbsp;Leanpath, the leader in enterprise food waste management, has announced a new backend integration with Nutritics to bridge the gap between menu planning and waste tracking.</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>This partnership establishes a powerful, two-way data flow that gives our shared clients a level of insight and accuracy that simply wasn’t possible before.&nbsp;It’s not just about tracking what gets thrown away; it’s about connecting that loss directly to your purchasing and preparation strategies.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>A Closed-Loop System</strong></h3>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>The concept is simple but effective: create a &#8220;closed-loop&#8221; system where food data management and waste reduction talk to each other<sup></sup>. By connecting Nutritics’ recipe management with Leanpath’s tracking, operators can finally see the full picture.</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>Here is how it works in practice:</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Item-Specific Monitoring</strong></h4>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>Detailed menu and pricing information from Nutritics is now imported directly into the Leanpath tracking platform<sup></sup>.</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>This solves a common headache for kitchen teams.&nbsp;Instead of generic waste categories, staff can track waste by the exact menu item<sup></sup>.&nbsp;Because the pricing data flows in with the item, you get immediate visibility into the associated cost, making the data much more financially relevant to your bottom line<sup></sup>.</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Operational Optimisation</strong></h4>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>Tracking waste is useful, but stopping it is better.&nbsp;With this integration, detailed waste data flows automatically back into Nutritics<sup></sup>.</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>By combining these &#8220;wasted food&#8221; insights with your existing menu management and purchasing modules, you can make smarter decisions.&nbsp;It becomes easier to adjust inventory orders, tweak prep quantities, or change menu schedules to stop waste from happening in the first place.</p>
</div>
