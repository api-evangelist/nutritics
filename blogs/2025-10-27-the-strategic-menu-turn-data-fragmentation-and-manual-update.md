<p>In the world of hospitality, the menu is far more than a list of dishes. It’s a living, breathing brand asset, one that shapes guest perception, operational efficiency, and compliance credibility. For marketing and operations leaders, it&#8217;s a critical touchpoint that impacts everything from brand reputation to customer safety. </p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>Yet, teams often struggle with fragmented data and manual updates, all while facing the constant pressure of changing legislation. As customer expectations for transparency rise, from calories to allergens, the risks of outdated or inconsistent menus grow exponentially.</p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Solving the Operational and Compliance Nightmare: Introducing a Single Source of Truth for Menu Data</h3>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<p>This is the problem Ten Kites was built to solve. Stuart Wilson, founder, recognised a clear need in the industry, catalysed by the 2014 allergen legislation, to create a unified, automated solution for menu management.   His vision was simple but transformative: one connected system that ensures every menu, everywhere, always tells the same, compliant story.<br /></p>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">For businesses searching for automated menu management software for restaurants, Ten Kites offers a precise, end-to-end workflow:</h3>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Connect Recipes: Ten Kites integrates with a brand’s existing recipe management system, ensuring that any change to a recipe is immediately captured and analyzed for compliance data.</li>



<li>Apply Brand Rules: We then apply the brand&#8217;s unique design and layout rules, guaranteeing consistency across all digital and print materials.</li>



<li>Publish Across Channels: With a single click, the updated, compliant, and on-brand menu is published everywhere it needs to be, ensuring you achieve menu compliance across all channels.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Tangible Results: Freeing Up Your Team</h3>
</div>

<div class="container--930 wysiwyg">

This not only frees up valuable time for marketing teams to focus on strategic initiatives but also keeps compliance in sync across channels.</p>
